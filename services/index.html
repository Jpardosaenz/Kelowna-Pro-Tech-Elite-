Frequently Asked Questions

Real questions from Kelowna vehicle owners. Straight answers.

Can I get a certified mechanic to come to my location in Kelowna?

Yes. A certified mechanic comes to your driveway, workplace, parking lot, or roadside in Kelowna — no tow required. You don’t bring the car to us. We arrive with professional diagnostic and repair tools, and the work happens at your location the same day.

Do I have to take my car to a shop for a proper diagnostic?

No. A complete professional diagnostic is performed at your location using dealer-grade scan tools, live data analysis, and schematic testing — the same process used in any certified shop. Most diagnostics are completed within 60 to 90 minutes on-site, without moving the vehicle.

Is the diagnostic fee applied toward the repair?

Yes. The diagnostic cost is credited toward the repair if you proceed with the work on the same visit. You are not charged twice. If you decide not to proceed, you pay for the diagnostic only — and leave with a documented answer about what your vehicle needs.

Can the mechanic fix my car the same day?

In most cases, yes. No-starts, electrical faults, sensor failures, and fluid issues are typically resolved on the same visit. Repairs requiring ordered parts may need a follow-up, but you get a clear answer and a realistic timeline the same day the mechanic arrives.

What does a pre-purchase inspection include?

A comprehensive mechanical and electrical assessment performed at the seller’s location before any money changes hands. Covers engine and fluid systems, transmission, brakes, suspension, electrical systems, and body condition. You receive a clear written verdict — what’s solid, what’s worn, and what would cost you money after the sale.

My check engine light is on. Can a mechanic diagnose it without a shop?

Yes. The root cause — not just the fault code — is confirmed on-site using professional scan tools and live data testing. Reading a code identifies the system; a real diagnostic confirms why it triggered. Most check engine diagnostics are completed at your location within 60 to 90 minutes.

Another shop couldn’t find the problem. Can your mechanic help?

Yes — this is one of the most common reasons people call us. Intermittent faults, misread codes, and symptoms that appear only under specific conditions require root-cause diagnostic work. We start from live data and schematic analysis, not from the previous shop’s guess. Over 55 verified clients have used this process.

Kelowna, West Kelowna, Lake Country, Peachland, Penticton, and Vernon. If you’re unsure whether your location is covered, call or text (250) 859-5467 and we’ll confirm directly. No automated system — you reach the mechanic. Service is fully mobile, so coverage extends to wherever the vehicle is located.

Can I watch the mechanic work on my car?

Yes — that’s the point of working this way. The diagnosis and repair happen at your location, in front of you. You can observe the process, ask questions at any point, and hear the findings before any work is approved. No back room. No waiting room. No surprises.

How much does a pre-purchase inspection cost?

We don’t publish prices — call or text (250) 859-5467 for a quote specific to your vehicle and location. What we can tell you: clients have used our inspection to identify hidden repair costs ranging from $2,000 to over $7,000 on vehicles the seller described as fine.
