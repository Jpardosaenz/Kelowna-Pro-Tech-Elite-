What should I check on my car before spring in Kelowna?
Spring is the right time to swap to summer tires, inspect brake pads after winter, check the undercarriage for road salt damage, test your battery, top up fluids, and make sure your AC is ready for summer heat. We handle all of this on-site at your location — same day, no shop required.
